A Suite of Features

Defibrillators

Record the properties, identifiers, and characteristics of your defibrillators. Enter just the essentials or up to a hundred data points based on your needs: electrodes, batteries, cabinet, terminal, and more. The full activity history of each device is accessible from its profile. You can also archive selected defibrillators to keep a traceable record.

Clients

Store client-related information and use their profiles to link them to defibrillators, contracts, rentals, tickets, maintenance tasks, and much more. Keeping their information up to date also helps streamline communications, whether manual or automated.

Maintenance

There are several ways to perform maintenance: either individually, by selecting a specific defibrillator, or in batches during a scheduled maintenance tour. During the visit, the technician has access to the device’s latest data and full history. They are then prompted to complete around ten key fields.

Service Tours

Technicians often travel to perform multiple maintenance tasks for one or more clients. Our tour planning system allows them to select the defibrillators needing attention, as well as any to be installed, and efficiently schedule everything into a single route.

Inventory

Record all items in your stock: electrodes, batteries, accessories, kits, spare parts, and more. Items can then be removed from stock, for instance during a maintenance visit. The used product is automatically deducted from inventory and marked accordingly.

Technicians

Whether you work with in-house or external technicians, register their profiles to give them direct access to perform maintenance and installations through your software.

Agreements

Store key data and references related to a contract established with a client to generate specific alerts and combine defibrillator options according to the rules defined in the agreement.

Compliance

The operational scope of defibrillators is sometimes well defined, sometimes more vague. Use our software to ensure compliance with essential obligations and anticipate future market regulations.

Tickets & Customer Relations

Strengthen relationships with your clients and never miss a request. Tickets can be generated from multiple sources: manually by you, or for example via a shareable link accessible through the reporting feature.

Revenue

Stabilize and increase your revenue with our financial tracking feature. Never miss a billing opportunity, whether related to contracts or maintenance activities. Agreement deadlines are clearly highlighted. Please note: we do not provide invoicing, quote, or contract generation tools.

Public Shareable Link

Encourage checks and tracking of defibrillators without frustrating or overwhelming your clients. Let them focus on their core business. Turn any visitor into a potential link in the chain of survival. Strengthen your presence and streamline support requests with this solution.

Collective and Artificial Intelligence

Whether it’s preventing failures, optimizing interventions, or enhancing the safety of your fleet, you have the data to manage your operations with precision and confidence.

How many sessions and users can I add?

The number of sessions is unlimited with our Mosaic plan. A single login and password are enough for all your sessions. With the Horizons plan, the rules differ: you can add up to 3 users, each representing a separate session. Additional fees apply to add more than 3 users.

Is the software accessible from a smartphone or tablet?

Yes, our entire software – especially the maintenance section – is accessible from any device, mobile or desktop. This is essential for technicians in the field, and meets the needs of convenience and mobility. While not mandatory, we recommend using our DefibeoTerminal device: an intuitive tablet, specially designed to support technicians' tasks and office operations thanks to native software integration.

Does the cost vary depending on the number of defibrillators?

No, our pricing is simple and transparent. Regardless of the plan you choose (Mosaic or Horizons), you can add an unlimited number of defibrillators. Unlock your growth!

What is the cancellation policy for a subscription?

No notice period, no hassle: contact us to cancel your subscription. Your software usage and billing period will end at the conclusion of the already-paid term. We currently do not offer prorated refunds.

Is it possible to create invoices, quotes, and contracts?

No. At Defibeo, we believe accounting and paralegal tasks should remain specialized professions. Many third-party services offer powerful solutions for all budgets. However, it is very easy to link data from these services directly to our software. For example, each defibrillator can be linked to a contract; each technician intervention can generate a financial entry to which you can associate an invoice, its status, and much more.

Is the software compliant with medical industry standards?

Yes. In the medical field, traceability, archiving, and history are essential. That’s exactly what Defibeo offers. Our team also provides continuous regulatory monitoring, both nationally and across Europe. We also integrate technical specifications provided by manufacturers, which are often more precise than institutional standards.

How can I find out if a promotion is available?

We regularly offer discounts through our partners, manufacturers, or during events, depending on your organization’s category. Feel free to contact us to check if a promotion is currently available.

Besides defibrillators, can I record accessories, terminals, cabinets, and other items?

Yes, Defibeo allows you to record all information related to parts and equipment associated with each defibrillator.

Can technician reports be generated as PDF files?

Yes, but only with the Horizons plan. With the Mosaic plan, reports can be viewed online, for example via a link shared with the client.

Does the software send alerts by SMS or email?

Yes, with the Horizons plan, alerts are sent by email. SMS notifications are also available but come with additional fees depending on your company and the volume. With the Mosaic plan, alerts are viewable directly within the software, particularly via the client’s dedicated page.

How can I meet my regulatory obligations regarding materiovigilance?

Our software includes features specifically designed for materiovigilance, in compliance with the latest national and European regulations. You benefit from automatic alerts, incident tracking, and the ability to generate reports that meet current requirements.
